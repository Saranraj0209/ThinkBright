Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our internship program

Who is eligible to apply for an internship?

+

Our internships are open to current students (undergraduate or graduate) and recent graduates (within 12 months of graduation). Some positions may have specific academic or skill requirements.

What is the application process?

+

Our process includes: 1) Online application, 2) Resume review, 3) Skills assessment (if applicable), 4) Virtual interview with hiring team, 5) Decision notification within 2-3 weeks of interview.

Can international students apply?

+

We welcome applications from international students who are eligible to work in their country of residence. For remote internships, we accept candidates worldwide.

What skills should I highlight in my application?

+

Focus on skills relevant to the position you're applying for. For technical roles, highlight coding languages, frameworks, and projects. For non-technical roles, emphasize relevant coursework, leadership experience, and transferable skills.
