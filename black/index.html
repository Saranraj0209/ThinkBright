About ThinkBright Edtech

Founded in 2024, ThinkBright Edtech has established itself as a premier educational institution focused on competitive exam preparation,providing interships and publishing books in India.

We operate Spark IAS Academy in partnership with Adda247, offer internship opportunities for college students, and publish high-quality study materials for various competitive exams.

Our Mission

To democratize quality education by making comprehensive exam preparation accessible and effective for all.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted partner in competitive exam preparation through innovation and excellence.
